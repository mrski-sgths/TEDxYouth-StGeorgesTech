Speaker Application

Do you have an idea worth sharing about EQUITY?

Then, we invite you to complete the speaker application below to be considered as a speaker for our event. We will be reviewing applications to ensure they meet TEDx guidelines and are in alignment with our theme. Those selected to continue will be invited to auditions on Tuesday, March 31 and Thursday, April 2 at 3 PM.

Please note that once you start the application, you will need to complete it as it can not be saved and continued later. Be sure to read all details in the application and answer all questions fully.
